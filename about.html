About TeaChai

Our Story

2010

Founded in a Small Kitchen

What started as a passion project in a home kitchen became the foundation of TeaChai.

2013

First Retail Location

Opened our first storefront in downtown Portland, introducing our unique blends to the public.

2016

National Distribution

Partnered with major retailers to bring TeaChai products to stores across the country.

2019

Sustainable Sourcing Initiative

Committed to 100% ethically sourced ingredients and eco-friendly packaging.

2023

Global Expansion

Launched international shipping to share TeaChai with tea lovers worldwide.
